Namek

Once in Times, it was well inhabited. But by now, only folks which are too weak or refuse to leave remain on this planet.

Story

First History - The forgotten Era

Namek is not an interesting Planet, which most of the folks soon realized. The Races are weak, the monsters too little and rest was too tidy. The Dragons were the first to leave, since then many Humans, Elves and other Races followed them. They all shared a secret together, the secret of world traveling. The unknown Power allowed them to leave Namek. Yet, those who left without a trace, left nothing for theirs and their knowlegde was forgotten.

The people were scared, all strong warriors and sages disappeared. The balance and order was collapsing. Namek would never be the same again. Many Monsters came and raided the lands and killed the defenseless. Before a complete disaster, a handful of people gathered together to bring new Order: one Nymph, an Elf, a Dragonborn and one Human. Those four Legends were Nameks last hope and the Pillars for the new Era

New Order

Those four Legends combined their strength to defeat the Monsters and saved Namek Citizens from extinction. The Monsters withdrew to a place, which is today known as the Sea of Death. Yet they were not friends, they just had the same goal. So they fought. The strongest of them should get the biggest Island, while the weakest got the smallest one.

Ancient Legends

The Elf

The ancient Elf was the weakest of the ancient Legends. At least this is believed so. They did not wanted to fight against any of the others. He gladly claimed the smallest Island and followed his goal. His was that the past events should not repeat themselves.

The Human

The ancient Human was Rouge, which walked in the dark and struck in the light. He wasn't really popular and not very kind. His goal was to create a harbor, in which even smugglers, thief and other criminals could feel safe and have freedom from strict laws.

The Nymph

The Nymph fought merciless, but was unable to defeat the great Dragonborn. It is not known if the Nymph planned all along, to separate herself from the other, or if this was because of frustration. Yet she used her water magic to distance her Island from the others. While the other more or less still sustained contact, the Nymph cut all threads with her former companions. It was narrated that, She was the meanest of them all and that she was racist towards landcreatures.

The Dragonborn

The ancient Dragonborn lived for the heat of the battle. He always improved his skills with fighting. But even he, the strongest man alive, wasn't able to defeat the tough of time. In order to remain his legacy, he created an tournament Island. The winner of this tournament should be the new lord of this Island and (theoretically) the King of the entire world.

Even today, the Power of the Dragonborn is unmatched.

Erasesment of the past

This was done by the Elf alone, but others weren't trying to stop him, so it is assumed that they all shared the same opinion.

The Elf gathered all the lore about world traveling and the old Namek and destroyed it in order to protect the new generation of Namek. Today, less than a handful of Books of the old Namek remain. Yet the secret of world traveling was erased completel

New Era - The golden Era

Soon a balance was made again. Those which had no intention to leave Namek, stayed and build a new System. And so the secret of world traveling was almost completely forgotten. By now, the old world Namek was forgotten and a new Era began.

Systems of the Islands

The new Era began with new laws. The most famous Rule in Mordenia was NO LAWS. In this Land was everything allowed and anything possible. Mordenia was the Island of the ancient Human.

Nirvana is the Land with the lowest population, next to Travenia. The word family "Kind" is not in the vocabulary of Nirvana's people. Nirvana bad habits came from ancient Nymph.

Llaveniss is the biggest Land on Namek. With the new Era, Llaveniss became a tournament Island. Llaveniss is a Land, where the strongest of Namek gather and fight for the Peak of the World, just like the ancient Dragonborn wanted.

Travenia is truly a boring Island in comparison to the others. The weakest of all gather here, with hopes to get stronger and succeed in their goals. The Elf setted on this Island. His invention was to create the roots of this world. Making Travenias people stronger, was a hedge that the events of the past should never be repeated.

The Sea of Death became its name due to the terrible Monsters who hide beneath this wicked waters. These Monsters wait for their prey, destroying even the biggest ships and kill even the strongest Warriors.

Picures

Dragons

The Story and disappears of the Dragons leaves many open questions. The gigantic winged lizards are known as the Dragons. The majestic Beasts left this world a very long time ago. It was a mystery, why the elder Dragons left Namek so sudden. They may thought the same as the other, that Namek was boring and weak. Yet, was this reason enough to disappear and leaving all their descendants back. Their Children's Eggs hatched and grew without their parents. What happened with the dragonslings, is known. Probably they all have died off, lack of protection and love.

Humans

"I am a Human" is a Quote which triggers often disgust at others. The remaining Humas are mostly outlaws and criminals. Of course, if the Humans still exist...

Landcreatures

The landcreatures of Namek are all spread across Namek. Their roots go far beyond the chronicles of Namek. Indeed, they almost extinct if the old legends not have been.

Nightcreatures

They are feared and hated by the most. As a result of this, their Kind is on the verge of extinction. The last Hotspot is the moon Village, in the west of Travenia. On these plains live the so-called Childs of Night. The planes are not often visited and well protected. However, if this Hotspot falls, the end of the Nightcreatures shall follow soon.

Watercreatures

Most of the Watercreatures live on Nirvana and these are not very friendly to other races. Their roots go far beyond the chronicles of Namek. Indeed, they almost extinct if the old legends not have been.

Demons

The Demon race is the most mysterious and evil race. They have neither loyalty nor any moral. They steal and kill from all, including themselves. Through the self-destructive behavior of the Demons, there would not have lasted long. Out of nowhere came the Demonking. The new king of the Demons, had the endless demands to claim the Lands of Namek. In Order to do so, he seeks people and artifacts with power.
