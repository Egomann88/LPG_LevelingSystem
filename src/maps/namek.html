Namek

Once in Times, it was well inhabited. But by now, only folks which are too weak or refuse to leave remain on this planet.

Story

Namek is not an intressting Planet, which most of the volks soon realised. The Races are weak, the monsters too little and rest was to tidy. The Dragons were the first to leave, since then many Humans, Elfes and other Races followed them. They all shared a secret together, the secret of worldtraveling. The unknown Power allowed them to leave Namek. Yet, thoose who left without a trace, left nothing for theirs.

Soon a balance was made again. Thoose which had no intention to leave Namek, stayed and builed an new System. And so the secret of worldtraveling was almost completely forgotten. By now, the old world Namek was forgotten and a new Era began.

The new Era began with new laws. The most famous Rule in Mordenia was NO LAWS. In this Land was everything allowed and anything possible. These Monsters wait for their prey, destroing even the biggest ships and kill even the stronges Warriors.

Nirvana is the Land with the lowest population next to Travenia. The wordfamily "Kind" is not in the vocabulary of Nirvana's people.

Llaveniss is the biggest Land on Namek. With the new Era, Llaveniss became a tounament Island. Llaveniss is a Land, where all of the strongest Races of Namek gather and fight for the Peak of the World.

The Sea of Death became its name due to the terrible Monsters who hide beneath this wicked waters.

Travenia is truly a boring Island in comparison to the others. The weakes of all gather here, with hopes to get stronger and succed in their goals.
