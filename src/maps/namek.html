Namek

Once in Times, it was well inhabited. But by now, only folks which are too weak or refuse to leave remain on this planet.

Story

Namek is not an interesting Planet, which most of the folks soon realized. The Races are weak, the monsters too little and rest was too tidy. The Dragons were the first to leave, since then many Humans, Elves and other Races followed them. They all shared a secret together, the secret of world traveling. The unknown Power allowed them to leave Namek. Yet, those who left without a trace, left nothing for theirs and their knowlegde was forgotten.

Soon a balance was made again. Those which had no intention to leave Namek, stayed and build a new System. And so the secret of world traveling was almost completely forgotten. By now, the old world Namek was forgotten and a new Era began.

The new Era began with new laws. The most famous Rule in Mordenia was NO LAWS. In this Land was everything allowed and anything possible.

Nirvana is the Land with the lowest population, next to Travenia. The word family "Kind" is not in the vocabulary of Nirvana's people.

Llaveniss is the biggest Land on Namek. With the new Era, Llaveniss became a tournament Island. Llaveniss is a Land, where the strongest of Namek gather and fight for the Peak of the World.

The Sea of Death became its name due to the terrible Monsters who hide beneath this wicked waters. These Monsters wait for their prey, destroying even the biggest ships and kill even the strongest Warriors.

Travenia is truly a boring Island in comparison to the others. The weakest of all gather here, with hopes to get stronger and succeed in their goals.
