Lorkarniya

This planet is the home and master creation of the gods. Everything on this planet consists of magic. Yet in the hidden corners where the gods' gaze does not fall, there are those who they should fear...

Story

Lorkarniya is a magical Planet created by the gods. Where the gods came from, nor what their mission was is unkown. However the have created many Plantes, yet Lorkarniya was their masterpice. The magic flows perfectly through the Lands. The Magic of Lorkarniya is also much stronger and purer than the magic of other Plantes.
