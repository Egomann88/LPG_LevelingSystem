Invest Points

Leben

  • Leben: +1
  • Mana: +10
  • Energie: +10

Magische Res

  • Magic System Picture
  • T1 Magic (blue bg) -> 3 Pt. to learn
  • T2 Magic (white bg) -> 4 Pts. to learn
  • For every connection to that Element -1 Pt. to learn
  • T1 Magic (blue bg) -> 1 Pt. at begining (upgrade)
  • T2 Magic (white bg) -> 2 Pts. at begining (upgrade)
  • + 1 Pt. to spend for any Magic Upgrade
  • Also, you can upgrade / level an Ability

Physische

  • 1 Pt. for any upgrade

Ges-Sinne

  • 1 Pt. for any upgrade
  • The max amout of Points musn't be over your charater level

Bewegung

  • 1 Pt. for any upgrade
  • The max amout of Points musn't be over your charater level
